## Phone Script Template

> "Hi, is this [Owner/Manager Name]? My name is Jonathon with Antigravity Digital Services. I'm calling because I just launched a free online directory specifically for auto shops in Schuylkill County, and your shop is already listed. I wanted to make sure your information is correct — do you have 30 seconds?"

After confirming info, transition:

> "Great. The reason I'm reaching out is [PERSONALIZED HOOK — see below]. We have a Featured Listing option that could help with that."

> "A Featured Listing puts your shop at the top of the page with photos of your shop, customer testimonials, and a 'Featured Partner' badge. It's $99 a month, no contracts, cancel anytime. The way I look at it — one repair job from a new customer who finds you through the directory, and the listing pays for itself for the rest of the year."

### Close

> "I'd like to offer you the first month free so you can see the results. Can I get you set up?"

## Objection Handlers

### "All my business is repeat customers / word of mouth."

> "That's great — it means your work speaks for itself. But here's the thing: repeat customers eventually move away, pass on, or sell their cars. You always need new customers coming in to replace the ones you lose naturally. A Featured Listing makes sure new residents and younger drivers can find you the way they search — online."

### "I don't need a fancy listing."

> "I totally get that. You've been doing fine without one. But think about it this way — your competitors who DO have an online presence are picking up the customers who search online first. Those are customers who might have come to you if they'd known you existed. For $99/month — less than the profit on a single oil change — you make sure nobody misses you."

### "I tried advertising before and it didn't work."

> "Most advertising is a spray-and-pray approach. This is different — the only people who see your listing are people actively searching for auto services in Schuylkill County. They already need what you sell. You're not paying to reach thousands of random people. You're paying to be visible to the 50 people a month who are searching for exactly what you do."

### "$99/month is too much."

> "I understand. Let me put it in perspective: one [repair/body job/tire sale] from a new customer who finds you on the directory covers the listing for [X months]. And there's no contract — you can cancel any time. I'm also offering the first month free so you can see the results before you spend a dime."

### "I'll think about it."

> "Absolutely, take your time. I'll send you an email with the details so you have everything in front of you. Just know that we're only offering the free first month to the first few shops that sign up — after that, it's $99 from day one."
